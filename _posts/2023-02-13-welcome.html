Aim

The aim of this project is to use Near Field Communication for transmitting wireless power (WPT) from a NFC transmitter (reader) to a receiver (tag). The receiver harvests the energy and use it for providing power to a sensors system.

Objectives

  1. Use WPT in Near field communications
  2. Energy Harvesting
  3. Green IoT (Create a battery less system)
  4. System works on short range distance with low cost sensors
  5. Monitor the humidity/temperature of the environment and the moisture of the soil
  6. Data transmission through NFC

Block Diagram

I will build a system that allow to measure and monitor the temperature/humidity pf t and the moisture of the soil through NFC. This can be useful for personal use at home or for monitoring plants in farms or greenhouses.

The system will be composed by:

  • NFC Reader (a smarthphone with NFC support)
  • Dynamic NFC Tag with Energy Harvester (ie M24LR) and Antenna
  • Low power microcontroller (ie ATTiny85)
  • Temperature/Humidity of the environment and the soil moisture sensors
