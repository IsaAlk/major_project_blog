Goal

A battery less sensors system to use in agriculture.

System able to measure the PH and moisture of the soil using sensors that are powered by Wireless Power Transfer though Near Field Communication.

Tasks

  • Design and Implement an NFC TAG with energy harvester
  • implement a circuit with a low power micro controller and PH and moisture sensors
  • assemble components
  • coding microcontroller
  • send data using NFC to an NFC reader
  • testing
